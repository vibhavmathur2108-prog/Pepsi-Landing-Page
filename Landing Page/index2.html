ABOUT US

PepsiCo is a leading multinational food and beverage company founded in 1965 through the merger of Pepsi-Cola and Frito-Lay. Headquartered in Purchase, New York, it operates in over 200 countries and serves billions of consumers worldwide.
Its popular brands include Pepsi, Mountain Dew, Lay's, Doritos and Kurkure. PepsiCo focuses on innovation, sustainability and delivering quality products globally through its extensive distribution network.
